Mangoes Classifier

Use images of Palmer or Tommy Atkins mangoes!

Our classifier was trained with images from 2 classes (Palmer and Tommy Atkins) and 90% of well classified images had a class confidence level greater than 79% for Palmer and 68% for Tommy Atkins.

Therefore, any image analyzed here with a confidence level lower than the one of its predicted class will be considered as "unrecognized" (example: if the classifier assigns an image a confidence level of 78% for the Palmer class, "unrecognized" will be displayed).
